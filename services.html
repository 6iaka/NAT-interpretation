Our Services

Comprehensive language solutions tailored to your specific needs

Document Translation

Certified translation services for official documents accepted by government agencies, educational institutions, courts, and immigration offices worldwide.

Official Document Translation

We provide certified translations for a wide range of official documents:

  • Birth certificates and death certificates
  • Passports and identification documents
  • Academic transcripts and diplomas
  • Marriage certificates and divorce decrees
  • Medical records and reports
  • Legal documents and contracts
  • Business documents and certificates
  • Immigration documents

Certification & Notarization: All translations include a certificate of accuracy and can be notarized upon request. Our certified translations are accepted by USCIS, universities, courts, and government agencies.

Translation Process

  • Submit your document via secure upload or in-person
  • Receive a detailed quote within 24 hours
  • Professional translation by certified linguists
  • Quality assurance review by second translator
  • Certification and notarization (if required)
  • Secure delivery via email or mail

Turnaround Times

  • Standard: 3-5 business days
  • Rush: 24-48 hours (additional fee)
  • Emergency: Same-day service available (subject to availability)

Interpretation Services

Professional interpretation services for medical, legal, educational, and business settings. Available in-person, over-the-phone, and via video conference.

Medical Interpretation

Certified medical interpreters ensure accurate communication between healthcare providers and patients, maintaining patient confidentiality and cultural sensitivity.

  • Doctor appointments and consultations
  • Hospital visits and emergency room interpretation
  • Medical procedures and surgeries
  • Mental health counseling sessions
  • Physical therapy and rehabilitation
  • Pharmacy consultations
  • Medical billing and insurance discussions

HIPAA Compliant: All our medical interpreters are trained in HIPAA regulations and maintain strict patient confidentiality.

Education Interpretation

Supporting students and families in educational settings with professional interpretation services for meetings, assessments, and school events.

  • Parent-teacher conferences
  • Individualized Education Program (IEP) meetings
  • School enrollment and registration
  • Special education assessments
  • Disciplinary hearings
  • Graduation ceremonies and school events
  • College admissions interviews

Educational Expertise: Our interpreters understand educational terminology and special education processes.

Court Interpretation

Certified court interpreters provide accurate, impartial interpretation for legal proceedings, maintaining the integrity of the judicial process.

  • Criminal court hearings and trials
  • Civil court proceedings
  • Depositions and sworn statements
  • Arbitration and mediation
  • Probation and parole meetings
  • Attorney-client consultations
  • Immigration court hearings

Certified & Sworn: Our court interpreters are certified and can provide sworn interpretation services as required by the court.

Conference Interpretation

Professional conference interpretation services for business meetings, international conferences, and large-scale events.

  • Simultaneous interpretation (with equipment)
  • Consecutive interpretation
  • Business meetings and negotiations
  • International conferences and conventions
  • Training sessions and workshops
  • Webinars and virtual events
  • Press conferences and media events

Equipment Available: We can provide interpretation equipment including headsets, microphones, and booths for large events.

Interpretation Modalities

  • In-Person: On-site interpretation at your location
  • Over-the-Phone (OPI): Immediate access to interpreters via phone
  • Video Remote (VRI): Secure video interpretation via computer or tablet
  • On-Demand: Same-day and emergency services available
