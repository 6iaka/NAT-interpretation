Our Expert Team

Our interpreters and translators are certified, experienced, and committed to providing accurate, culturally sensitive language services. Meet the professionals who make it all possible.

Lead Specialist

Aminata

Certified Interpreter & Translator

With extensive expertise in interpretation and translation services, Aminata brings a wealth of knowledge and cultural sensitivity to every project. Her commitment to accuracy, professionalism, and client satisfaction has made her a trusted partner for medical facilities, legal offices, educational institutions, and businesses throughout Maine and beyond. She specializes in medical interpretation (HIPAA certified), court interpretation (federally certified), and certified document translation.

Maria Rodriguez

Lead Medical Interpreter & Certified Translator

Maria brings over 15 years of experience in medical interpretation, specializing in Spanish-English language pairs. She is HIPAA certified and has extensive experience in hospital settings, emergency rooms, and specialized medical consultations. Maria holds a Master's degree in Translation Studies and is certified by the American Translators Association (ATA).

Ahmed Hassan

Certified Court Interpreter

Ahmed is a federally certified court interpreter with expertise in Arabic-English interpretation. He has provided interpretation services for criminal and civil court proceedings, depositions, and immigration hearings throughout Maine. Ahmed is certified by the Administrative Office of the U.S. Courts and maintains strict adherence to the interpreter code of ethics.

Sophie Chen

Education Interpreter & Document Translator

Sophie specializes in educational interpretation and academic document translation. With a background in education and fluency in Mandarin, Cantonese, and English, she has extensive experience with IEP meetings, parent-teacher conferences, and translating academic transcripts and diplomas. Sophie is certified by the National Association of Judiciary Interpreters and Translators (NAJIT).

Jean-Pierre Dubois

Conference Interpreter & Business Translator

Jean-Pierre is a professional conference interpreter specializing in French-English language pairs. He provides simultaneous and consecutive interpretation for international business meetings, conferences, and large-scale events. With over 12 years of experience, Jean-Pierre has worked with Fortune 500 companies and international organizations. He holds certification from the International Association of Conference Interpreters (AIIC).

Why Our Team Stands Out

What makes our team exceptional? Here are the key qualities that set us apart.

Certified & Qualified

All our interpreters and translators hold professional certifications from recognized organizations including ATA, NAJIT, and federal court certification. We maintain continuing education requirements to stay current with industry standards.

Background Checked

Every team member undergoes comprehensive background checks and security clearances, especially important for court and medical interpretation work where confidentiality and trust are paramount.

Cultural Competency

Our team members are not just linguistically fluent—they understand cultural nuances, regional dialects, and context-specific terminology that ensures accurate and culturally appropriate communication.

Specialized Expertise

Each interpreter specializes in specific fields (medical, legal, education, business) ensuring they understand the terminology and protocols unique to each setting.
